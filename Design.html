Software: Adobe XD, Procreate

In this day and age many things are being switching to digital formats, and in many ways that is a good thing, it is much easier to make things more acessable, intuitive, and cleaner in a digital format. However this isnt always case as many of us have found. For me I regularly found that many restaurants did a poor job transfering their menus and ordering proccess on to devices, especially on mobile devices such as phones. Many menus where hard to read and navigate, had dated designs, or simply weren't designed for use on moblie devices.

For this project I set out to design a more intuitive mobile device menu design. This menu was designed to be viewed as a wed page or an app, its focuse is on easy navigation, readability, and user convieiance. One of the down side to the classic paper meun is space, there is only so much space that can be desinated to an item, with the digital format an infinite amout of space can be given. This allows detains that would normaly be available to be displayed.

All logos, icons, and titleings were designed by me.
