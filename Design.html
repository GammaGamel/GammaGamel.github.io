Software: Adobe XD, Procreate

In the contemporary landscape, there is a notable trend toward digitalization across various domains, presenting numerous benefits. Transitioning to digital formats often enhances accessibility, intuitiveness, and overall cleanliness of content. However, challenges persist despite these advantages, as many have experienced firsthand. Personally, I have observed that numerous restaurants struggle with the transition of their menus and ordering processes to digital platforms, particularly on mobile devices. These digital menus frequently suffer from readability issues, outdated designs, and inadequate optimization for mobile interfaces.

Motivated by these observations, I embarked on a project aimed at designing a more intuitive mobile menu interface. This interface, suitable for both web and app platforms, prioritizes seamless navigation, enhanced readability, and user-centric design. Notably, one of the key advantages of digital menus lies in their virtually limitless capacity for content presentation, overcoming the spatial constraints inherent in traditional paper menus.

I take pride in having personally crafted all logos, icons, and titling elements associated with this project.

How to Duck Poster

Software: Procreate

The above poster was created to inform the new cohorts in GIMM, in a light-hearted manner, of the rules and etiquette when "ducking". In GIMM, it is a part of the culture to leave and hide ducks; however, as the practice has grown in popularity, the need for guidance was apparent. It was my goal to communicate proper conduct, answer unaddressed questions, and give visual examples as to what is and isn't appropriate.
